Week 6: Fun Experience

Posted by Curious Lizzy (2 Comments)

This week made us acquire new skills.




We are tasked to get the serial numbers of the transceiver, switch, and UPS in College of Science and College of Nursing.



I was assigned to transmit the info using walki-talkie.



The next task we performed was intalling cctv, checking connections, creating LAN cables according to standard, and configuring servers.


A selfie after all the tasks!




After that, we installed SSD to the computers in ICTO. We learned how to change it and configure them.



In our last day of OJT, we grabbed the opportunity for picture taking! This whole experience made us discover more about ourselves and I will treasure it forever!
