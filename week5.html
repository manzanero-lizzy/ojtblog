Week 5: Sper busy week

Posted by Curious Lizzy (2 Comments)

We became facilitators of an event and it was fantastic!



The first two days of the week, we practiced accessing database and implementing queries. Ma'am Emerald guided us and gave us advices in database designing and implementation.



The CSIT department conducted an event as part of the Extension Services of Bicol University. The workshop is about using MS Excel for the barangay secretaries of Camalig, Albay. The three-day training is conducted in CS Building 2 and we, the interns became the facilitators of the event.
