Week 3: Exploring

Posted by Curious Lizzy (2 Comments)

I got to explore a lot of new things this week.

We started this week by celebrating the birthday of our co-intern. It was a fun day for us. Later that week, we tried something new.

Bicol University was preparing for graduation and ICTO will ensure that the livestreaming will be smooth. As an intern of ICTO, we witnessed the intallation process in BU Granstand.


I helped in arranging the wires and ensuring the connectivity to wi-fi. We also watched Sir Carlo on how to do it.


There are only four interns that helped them in preparing the grandstand for the graduation.


This is also my first time using a walkie-talkie. The whole experience was amazing. We got to talk to our supervisors and learn from them.
